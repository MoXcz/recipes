Sopa de Tortilla

Image obtained from here

A simple, yet delicious, soup

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Water
  • Chipotle
  • Tomato Broth
  • Garlic
  • A pot

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Blend 3 tomatoes, 2 garlic cloves, 2-3 chipotles and tomato broth
  3. Boil the mixture and add water
  4. Wait around 30 minutes
